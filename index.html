WELCOME TO BONDO SQUARE About the Bar

Not just a bar — a community spot where games, food, music and friendship blend into unforgettable nights.

Bondo Square Sports Bar is designed for sports lovers, food enthusiasts and good-vibes-only crowds. Whether you’re celebrating a big win, unwinding after work or planning a special date, this is your home base.
